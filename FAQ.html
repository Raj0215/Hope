FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Please reach us at info@hopeaesthetics.in if you cannot find an answer to your question.

What is FUE Hair Transplant?

Follicular unit extraction (FUE) is a hair transplant technique that involves extracting individual hair follicles from your skin, usually the back or side of the head, and transplanting them to another part of your body where hair is weaker or nonexistent.

Is FUE Hair Transplant a Painful procedure?

FUE Hair Transplant is generally not considered a painful procedure. Local anesthesia is used during the process to minimize discomfort.

Are there any SCARS left behind after the procedure?

With FUE Hair Transplant, scars are minimal and typically less noticeable compared to other hair transplant techniques.

What are the benefits of FUE Hair Transplant?

Some benefits of FUE Hair Transplant include minimal scarring, quicker recovery time, and a natural-looking result.

Am I a suitable candidate for this procedure?

Most individuals with hair loss concerns are suitable candidates for FUE Hair Transplant. Consultation with a qualified physician is recommended to determine eligibility.

How Long Does the FUE Procedure Take?

The duration of the FUE procedure varies depending on the number of grafts required, but it typically takes between 4 to 8 hours.

What is the Recovery Time After FUE Hair Transplant?

Recovery time is relatively short. Most patients can return to work within 1-2 days, with full recovery within a week. It's important to follow post-operative care instructions to ensure proper healing.

When Will I See the Results of My FUE Hair Transplant?

Initial hair growth can be seen within 3-4 months, with more significant results visible around 6-9 months. Full results can take up to a year.

Are the Results of FUE Hair Transplant Permanent?

Yes, the transplanted hair is typically permanent as it is taken from areas resistant to hair loss. However, it's important to note that ongoing hair loss may occur in non-transplanted areas.

What Should I Expect During My Consultation for FUE Hair Transplant?

During your consultation, the doctor will assess your hair loss, discuss your expectations, explain the procedure, and determine if you are a suitable candidate for FUE.
