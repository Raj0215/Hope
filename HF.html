High Frequency Treatment

What Is High Frequency Comb Treatment?

High Frequency Comb treatment is a non-invasive procedure that uses gentle electrical currents to stimulate hair follicles and enhance scalp health. This therapy increases blood circulation to the scalp, oxygenates the hair follicles, and promotes hair growth by delivering a low-level electrical current through the comb. It's an effective method for treating hair loss, dandruff, and other scalp issues.

The treatment is particularly beneficial for those experiencing hair thinning or hair loss due to daily stress, poor dietary habits, or scalp conditions. The High Frequency Comb helps to revitalize the scalp, reduce hair shedding, and improve the overall health of your hair.


Why Choose High Frequency Comb Treatment?

High Frequency Comb treatment offers several benefits that make it an excellent choice for those looking to improve their hair health. Here are some key reasons to choose this treatment:

  Improves blood circulation to the scalp, promoting hair growth.

  Helps reduce dandruff and scalp irritation.

  Strengthens hair strands, reducing breakage and thinning.

  Non-invasive, pain-free, and suitable for all hair types.

  Promotes overall scalp health, making hair more resilient.


Procedure

The High Frequency Comb treatment typically involves a few straightforward steps that ensure optimal results:

  The treatment begins with a thorough cleansing of the scalp to remove any dirt or oils.

  A high-frequency comb is then passed over the scalp, emitting a low-level electrical current that stimulates blood flow and oxygenates the hair follicles.

  This process helps to rejuvenate the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

  The treatment is non-invasive and typically takes about 15-20 minutes.


Experience Post-Treatment:


1-2 Weeks: Initial improvements in scalp health, such as reduced dandruff and a fresher, more invigorated feeling, may be noticed.

1-2 Months: You may observe reduced hair shedding and initial signs of new hair growth or increased hair thickness.

3-6 Months: Continued improvements in hair density and scalp health, with more significant hair regrowth becoming apparent.

Procedure Frequency: Sessions are typically scheduled weekly or bi-weekly, depending on the individual's needs and the severity of hair loss.

Ongoing Maintenance: To sustain the results, occasional maintenance sessions may be recommended.

  Stimulates hair growth and strengthens hair strands.
