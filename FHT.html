Full Head Transplant

What Is Full Head Transplant?

A Full Head Hair Transplant is a surgical procedure aimed at restoring hair to areas of the scalp that are thinning or balding. It involves the relocation of hair follicles from a donor site, usually the back or sides of the head, to the recipient areas where hair loss has occurred. The goal is to achieve a natural-looking, fuller head of hair.


How Does It Work?

  Donor Hair Extraction : Hair follicles are harvested from a donor area, typically where hair is still plentiful. This can be done using main method FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction)

  Preparation of Recipient Site : Tiny incisions are made in the recipient area to create sites for the transplanted follicles. These incisions are strategically placed to mimic natural hair growth patterns.

  Transplantation : Hair follicles are carefully placed into the incisions on the recipient site. The orientation and angle of the follicles are adjusted to ensure a natural appearance.

  Recovery and Growth : The transplanted hair will initially shed as part of the natural hair growth cycle. New hair growth typically starts within a few months, with full results visible in about 9 to 12 months.


Benefits

  Restores Hair Density : Provides a fuller, thicker head of hair in areas affected by thinning or baldness.

  Natural Appearance : Designed to create a natural-looking hairline and hair growth pattern.

  Long-Term Solution : Offers a permanent solution to hair loss, as transplanted hair follicles are typically resistant to the effects of DHT (the hormone responsible for hair loss).


Experience During the Procedure


The Full Head Hair Transplant procedure typically lasts between 4 to 8 hours, depending on the extent of the transplant. Patients are usually given local anesthesia to numb the scalp, so the procedure itself is relatively painless. Some discomfort may be felt during recovery, which can be managed with prescribed medications. Most patients can return to their normal activities within a few days, although full recovery and optimal hair growth can take several months.
