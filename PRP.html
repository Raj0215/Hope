ADVANCED PRP +

What Is Advanced PRP +?

Advanced PRP therapy leverages the benefits of microneedling, a technique that serves as an alternative to the traditional syringe for applying platelet-rich plasma (PRP). In this approach, microneedling creates controlled microchannels in the skin's surface, which not only help the PRP to spread more uniformly but also improve its absorption into the deeper layers of the skin. This precise method is generally less painful compared to conventional techniques, minimizes downtime, and often leads to superior results, particularly in areas like skin rejuvenation and hair growth.

By integrating microneedling with PRP, the treatment stimulates the body’s natural healing processes. The microchannels act as a pathway for PRP, which contains growth factors that encourage the repair and regeneration of tissues. This combination effectively enhances the healing properties present in your own blood, leading to more noticeable and lasting improvements in the treated areas. Whether for improving skin texture, reducing the appearance of scars, or promoting hair growth, this advanced therapy offers a holistic and efficient solution for rejuvenation.

Procedure

In most cases, you will require four to six sessions 4-6 weeks apart. After that, you might need a couple of maintenance sessions according to your requirements.

  Doctor extracts approximately 50 ml of blood from your arm.

  Then the blood is kept in a centrifuge for separation. The centrifuge spins the blood at a very high speed to separates platelet-poor plasma, platelet-rich plasma, and red platelets from the blood sample.

  Separated platelet-rich plasma is collected in a syringe by the doctor.

  We use iodine or alcohol to clean your affected area of the scalp.

  Then hair transplant surgeon injects the Platelet-rich plasma into the affected area.


Experience Post-Treatment:


1-2 Months: Initial signs of improvement, such as reduced scalp sensitivity and minor changes in hair texture, may begin to appear.

2-3 Months: Early signs of hair growth, such as tiny new hairs or thicker hair strands, may start to become noticeable.

3-6 Months: Optimal results are typically visible as more significant hair regrowth and improved hair density are seen. Collagen production and hair follicle rejuvenation continue during this period.

Procedure Frequency: PRP sessions are usually scheduled every 1-2 months. The full treatment process may span up to 6 months, depending on the number of sessions recommended.

Ongoing Maintenance: Additional sessions might be needed for sustained results or further enhancement of hair growth.

How do daily habits cause hair fall, and what treatments can help?

Hair fall due to daily activities such as excessive hairstyling, frequent use of heat tools, tight hairstyles, and harsh chemical treatments can weaken hair strands and damage the scalp, leading to increased shedding and thinning. Stressful routines, inadequate sleep, and poor dietary habits also contribute to hair loss by disrupting the natural hair growth cycle. Advanced PRP therapy offers a solution by harnessing the body's own regenerative properties to repair and strengthen hair follicles. The treatment, especially when combined with microneedling, targets the affected areas, stimulating hair regrowth and improving scalp health for more resilient and healthier hair.
