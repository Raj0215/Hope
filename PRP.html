ADVANCED PRP +

What Is Advanced PRP +?

Advanced PRP therapy combines the benefits of microneedling with platelet-rich plasma (PRP) for enhanced hair rejuvenation. Microneedling creates tiny channels in the scalp, allowing PRP to penetrate more deeply and effectively. This method allows you to quickly resume your daily activities and produces better results, especially for promoting hair growth and improving overall hair health.


Why choose Advanced PRP + therapy?

In today’s world, increasing stress levels are a major concern, often leading to health issues like hair loss. Hair fall can occur at any age due to factors such as hormonal imbalances, stress, pollution, nutritional deficiencies, health issues, or genetics. While you may have tried oils and lotions, these remedies might not effectively address the problem. For a long-lasting solution to restore your youthful appearance, consider Advanced PRP + treatment at Hope Aesthetics International, a non-surgical hair restoration option.


How do daily habits cause hair fall, and what treatments can help?

Daily activities like excessive hairstyling, heat tools, tight hairstyles, and harsh chemicals can weaken hair and damage the scalp, leading to increased shedding. Stress, inadequate sleep, and poor diet also disrupt the hair growth cycle. Advanced PRP + therapy, especially when combined with microneedling, uses the body's regenerative properties to repair and strengthen hair follicles, stimulate regrowth, and improve scalp health for more resilient hair.


Procedure

Usually, you’ll need four to six sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart, with a few maintenance sessions as required.

  Blood Extraction: Approximately 6 to 8 ml of blood is drawn from your arm.

  Centrifugation: The blood is placed in a centrifuge, which spins it at high speed to separate platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from the other components.

  PRP Collection: The PRP is collected in a syringe by the doctor.

  Scalp Preparation: The affected area of your scalp is cleaned with iodine or alcohol.

  PRP Application: The hair transplant surgeon microneedles the PRP over the targeted area of the scalp.


Experience Post-Treatment:


1-2 Months: Initial improvements like reduced scalp sensitivity and minor texture changes.

2-3 Months: Noticeable early signs of hair growth, such as new tiny hairs or thicker strands.

3-6 Months: Significant hair regrowth and improved density, with ongoing follicle rejuvenation.

Procedure Frequency: Sessions every 1 month, with full treatment lasting up to 6 months.

Ongoing Maintenance: Additional sessions may be needed for sustained or enhanced results.


Advantages Of Advanced PRP + Hair Treatment

  No Scarring: The procedure does not result in scarring.

  Safe: Uses your own blood cells, ensuring safety.

  Minimally Unpleasant: Considered the least unpleasant among hair restoration methods.

  Increases Hair Growth: Stimulates blood flow to the scalp's hair follicles.

  Cost-Effective: Generally less expensive than alternative treatments.

  Regulates Hair Growth: Helps regulate the hair growth cycle.

  No Negative Outcomes: The procedure has no known negative outcomes.
