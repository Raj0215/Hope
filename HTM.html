Hair Transplant For Men

What Is Hair Transplant For Men?

A Hair Transplant for Men is a surgical procedure designed to address male pattern baldness or other types of hair loss. It involves relocating healthy hair follicles from a donor area (usually the back or sides of the head) to thinning or bald areas of the scalp. The goal is to restore a fuller head of hair with a natural appearance.


How Does It Work?

  Donor Hair Extraction : Hair follicles are harvested from a donor area, typically where hair is still plentiful. This can be done using method FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) Individual hair follicles are extracted directly from the scalp using a tiny punch tool.

  Recipient Site Preparation : Small incisions are made in the recipient area to prepare for the transplanted follicles, ensuring they align with natural hair growth patterns.

  Transplantation : Hair follicles are carefully placed into the incisions. The orientation and angle are adjusted for a natural look.

  Recovery and Growth : Transplanted hair may shed initially, but new growth typically begins within a few months, with full results visible in 9 to 12 months.


Benefits

  Natural-Looking Results : Creates a realistic hairline and blends with existing hair for a natural look.

  Permanent Solution : Transplanted hair follicles grow indefinitely, unlike temporary solutions such as wigs or topical treatments.

  Enhanced Appearance : Restores hair density, improving overall facial aesthetics and boosting confidence.

  Customizable : Tailored to individual needs and preferences, matching your desired hair style and coverage.


Experience During the Procedure


The procedure generally lasts 4 to 8 hours, depending on the extent of the transplant. Local anesthesia is used to minimize discomfort. While some mild discomfort and swelling may occur post-procedure, recovery is typically quick, with most people returning to normal activities within a few days. Full results will become evident in several months as new hair grows in.
