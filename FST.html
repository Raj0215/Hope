Follicle Stimulating Therapy

What Is Follicle Stimulating Therapy?

Follicle Stimulating Therapy (FST) is an advanced treatment designed to enhance hair growth and improve follicle health. This therapy utilizes a combination of techniques and technologies to stimulate dormant hair follicles, promote new hair growth, and strengthen existing hair. By applying targeted treatments such as growth factors, nutrients, and regenerative techniques, FST aims to rejuvenate the scalp and optimize the conditions for hair follicles to thrive. This non-invasive procedure is suitable for individuals experiencing thinning hair or early stages of hair loss and offers a promising solution for achieving fuller and healthier hair.


Why Choose Follicle Stimulating Therapy?

Effective for Thinning Hair: Targets and revitalizes hair follicles to combat thinning and early-stage hair loss.

Non-Surgical: Provides a non-invasive solution with minimal discomfort and no downtime.

Enhances Follicle Health: Improves the overall health of hair follicles, promoting stronger and healthier hair growth.

Quick and Convenient: Sessions are generally short, fitting easily into a busy schedule.

Personalized Treatment: Tailored to meet individual needs and hair loss conditions for optimal results.

Follicle Stimulating Therapy is an ideal choice for those seeking an effective and non-invasive treatment to address hair thinning and loss while enhancing overall follicle health.


Procedure

The Follicle Stimulating Therapy procedure involves several key steps to ensure effective treatment:

  Consultation: Assessment to determine the suitability and customize the treatment plan based on individual needs.

  Preparation: Scalp cleansing and preparation to ensure optimal absorption of therapeutic agents.

  Application: Delivery of growth factors, nutrients, or regenerative substances directly to the scalp.

  Stimulating Techniques: Use of advanced techniques such as microneedling or laser therapy to enhance the efficacy of the treatment.

  Post-Treatment Care: Providing instructions for aftercare to maximize results and support the healing process.


Experience Post-Treatment:

1-2 Months: Initial signs of improved hair texture and reduced thinning may become noticeable.

2-3 Months: Early signs of new hair growth and enhanced follicle health may be observed.

3-6 Months: Continued improvement in hair density and strength; noticeable results in hair regrowth.

Procedure Frequency: Typically requires multiple sessions, with follow-ups scheduled based on individual progress.

Ongoing Maintenance: Regular treatments may be recommended to sustain results and support long-term hair health.


Advantages Of Follicle Stimulating Therapy

  Non-Invasive: Offers a non-surgical approach to improve hair growth and follicle health.

  Enhanced Follicle Health: Boosts the health and function of hair follicles for better growth.

  Quick Recovery: Minimal downtime with a fast recovery period.

  Customizable Treatment: Tailored to individual needs and hair loss conditions.

  Improved Hair Growth: Stimulates natural hair growth processes for fuller and healthier hair.
