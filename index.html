Advanced PRP +
Therapy

Advanced PRP+ Therapy is an innovative hair treatment that leverages the regenerative potential of Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) to combat hair loss and stimulate hair growth. During the procedure, a small sample of your blood is drawn and processed in a centrifuge to concentrate the platelets and growth factors.

Follicular Unit Extraction

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) is a minimally invasive hair restoration procedure where individual hair follicles are extracted from the donor area and transplanted to thinning or balding areas of the scalp. This technique ensures natural-looking results with minimal scarring and a faster recovery time compared to traditional hair transplant methods.

Advanced GFC +
Therapy

Advanced GFC+ Therapy hair treatment harnesses the power of Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC) to combat hair loss and promote hair growth. During the procedure, a small amount of your blood is drawn and processed to isolate the growth factors. These concentrated growth factors are then injected into the scalp to stimulate hair follicles, enhance hair density, and improve overall hair health.

What is FUE Hair Transplant?

Follicular unit extraction (FUE) is a hair transplant technique that involves extracting individual hair follicles from your skin, usually the back or side of the head, and transplanting them to another part of your body where hair is weaker or nonexistent.

Is FUE Hair Transplant a Painful procedure?

FUE Hair Transplant is generally not considered a painful procedure. Local anesthesia is used during the process to minimize discomfort.

Are there any SCARS left behind after the procedure?

With FUE Hair Transplant, scars are minimal and typically less noticeable compared to other hair transplant techniques.

What are the benefits of FUE Hair Transplant?

Some benefits of FUE Hair Transplant include minimal scarring, quicker recovery time, and a natural-looking result.

Am I a suitable candidate for this procedure?

Most individuals with hair loss concerns are suitable candidates for FUE Hair Transplant. Consultation with a qualified physician is recommended to determine eligibility.
