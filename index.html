Advanced PRP Therapy

Advanced PRP Therapy (PRF) hair treatment harnesses the regenerative power of your own blood plasma to combat hair loss and promote hair growth. During the procedure, blood is drawn from your arm and processed in a centrifuge to separate the platelet-rich plasma (PRP) and fibrin.

Follicular Unit Extraction

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) hair transplant is a modern and popular technique for hair restoration. In this procedure, individual hair follicles are extracted from a donor area, usually the back or sides of the scalp, and then implanted into the balding or thinning areas.

Advanced Glycation End

Advanced Glycation End (AGEs) play a significant role in the development and progression of various age-related diseases, including diabetes and cardiovascular conditions. Glycation is a process where sugar molecules bind to proteins or lipids without enzymatic control, forming harmful AGEs.

What is FUE Hair Transplant?

Follicular unit extraction (FUE) is a hair transplant technique that involves extracting individual hair follicles from your skin, usually the back or side of the head, and transplanting them to another part of your body where hair is weaker or nonexistent.

Is FUE Hair Transplant a Painful procedure?

FUE Hair Transplant is generally not considered a painful procedure. Local anesthesia is used during the process to minimize discomfort.

Are there any SCARS left behind after the procedure?

With FUE Hair Transplant, scars are minimal and typically less noticeable compared to other hair transplant techniques.

What are the benefits of FUE Hair Transplant?

Some benefits of FUE Hair Transplant include minimal scarring, quicker recovery time, and a natural-looking result.

Am I a suitable candidate for this procedure?

Most individuals with hair loss concerns are suitable candidates for FUE Hair Transplant. Consultation with a qualified physician is recommended to determine eligibility.
