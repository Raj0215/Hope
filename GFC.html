Advanced GFC +

What Is Advanced GFC +?

Advanced GFC+ Therapy combines lab-made concentrated GFC with microneedling to enhance hair rejuvenation. The process involves extracting a small blood sample, mixing it with concentrated GFC, and then microneedling this enriched mixture into the scalp. This approach stimulates cellular repair, boosts collagen production, and rejuvenates hair follicles, leading to improved hair growth and density. Known for its significant, lasting results with minimal recovery time, Advanced GFC+ offers an effective solution for promoting hair regrowth and overall scalp health.

What daily habits contribute to hair loss, and how can it be prevented?

Daily routines can significantly impact hair loss, often due to poor diet, excessive heat styling, tight hairstyles, and stress, which weaken hair follicles and disrupt natural growth. Smoking, inadequate scalp care, and lack of sleep further exacerbate the issue. To address this, maintain a balanced diet, practice gentle hair care, manage stress, get enough sleep, and stay hydrated. Procedure

For Advanced Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC) therapy, the process involves:

  Blood Sample Collection: A small blood sample is drawn from the patient.

  Centrifugation: The blood is centrifuged to separate plasma components.

  Growth Factor Enrichment: Deficient nutrients are added to the plasma.

  Plasma Concentration: The enriched plasma is centrifuged again for optimal concentration.

  Microneedling: The concentrated plasma is applied to the desired area using microneedling.


Experience Post-Treatment:


1-2 Months: Initial improvements like reduced scalp sensitivity and minor texture changes.

2-3 Months: Noticeable early signs of hair growth, such as new tiny hairs or thicker strands.

3-6 Months: Significant hair regrowth and improved density, with ongoing follicle rejuvenation.

Procedure Frequency: Sessions every 1 month, with full treatment lasting up to 6 months.

Ongoing Maintenance: Additional sessions may be needed for sustained or enhanced results.
